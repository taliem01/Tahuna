33 Lomond Lodge Motel & Apartments

This property is 4 minutes walk from the beach. 33 Lomond Lodge Motel & Apartments is located in the centre of Queenstown. It offers affordable accommodation, 50 m from the lake front and gondola. Free Wi-Fi and free parking is available. Lomond Lodge provides comfortable studio rooms, as well as fully equipped apartments. All units have kitchen facilities and satellite TV. Most rooms have panoramic views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables Mountains. 33 Lomond Lodge Motel is a short walk to many restaurants and shops. The staff can arrange excursions and day trips. The property is a 10-minute drive from Queenstown Airport. Coronet Peak is a 20-minute drive away and The Remarkables ski area is a 40-minute drive away.


The Lodges

This property is 6 minutes walk from the beach. Located opposite Lake Wakatipu, The Lodges feature accommodation with a private balcony offering stunning lake and mountain views. Free parking is available on site. The Lodges are 10 minutes’ walk from Queenstown Gardens and the Queenstown Gondola. The Arrowtown and Coronet Peak ski fields are both within 30 minutes’ drive. Queenstown Airport is 8 km away and The Remarkables ski area is a 40-minute drive. The tour desk books local activities such as jet boating, bungee jumping and helicopter flights. Ski storage and laundry facilities are available on site.


Colonial Village Motel

Boasting rooms with stunning mountain and lake views, Colonial Village Motel is just a 5-minute walk from Lake Wakatipu. Free parking is available. A fully equipped kitchenette with an oven, stove, microwave and fridge is standard in all rooms. After a day out you can relax on the terrace or watch TV. Central Queenstown is just a 10-minute walk and Skycity Casino is a 20-minute walk away. Bring your bicycle and enjoy a ride around Lake Wakatipu where you can stop for a coffee at the Frankston Marina.


Bella Vista Queenstown

Bella Vista Motel is within walking distance to the Queenstown city centre, the Skyline Gondala and Lake Wakatipu. It offers rooms with beautiful mountain views. Guests enjoy 5GB of free WiFi per day. The studios and apartments at Bella Vista are serviced daily. All studios have kitchenettes, which include tea/coffee making facilities, a refrigerator, microwave and toaster. Some rooms have full cooking facilities. Guests can enjoy in-room movies. The staff at Bella Vista Motel Queenstown offer a comprehensive, free booking service for many local activities, including jet boating, skydiving and cruises on the Milford and Doubtful Sounds.


Blue Peaks Lodge

This property is 4 minutes walk from the beach. Just 5 minutes’ walk from Lake Wakatipu, Blue Peaks Lodge offers heated rooms with satellite TV. The property offers free parking and 500mb of free Wi-Fi per stay. Blue Peaks Lodge Queenstown is 3 minutes’ walk from Queenstown Gardens, and 10 minutes’ drive from the popular Onsen Hot Pools. Queenstown Airport is a 15-minute drive away. A refrigerator, tea and coffee maker and ironing facilities are standard in all rooms. Most rooms also have a separate seating area and a kitchenette with a microwave. The tour desk can book jet boating trips and tours to Milford Sound. Coronet Peak ski area is a 22-minute drive away and The Remarkables Ski Area is a 35-minute drive away
