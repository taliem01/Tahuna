Copthorne Hotel & Apartments Queenstown Lakeviews

Copthorne Hotel is located in Queenstown, a 10-minute walk from the resort centre. This 4-star hotel offers spacious accommodation and great views over Lake Wakatipu and the mountains. There are 85 modern guest rooms in total at the Copthorne Hotel. Choose between superior rooms with private courtyards or private balconies or the large 2-bedroom apartments, which have a spacious living area. Apartments have a lounge and a full kitchen


Hilton Queenstown Resort & Spa

Situated on the shores of Lake Wakatipu, Hilton Queenstown Resort & Spa boasts 5-star amenities including an indoor pool, a fitness centre and a day spa. The elegant rooms and suites feature a gas fireplace. Hilton Queenstown Resort is a 5-minute drive from Queenstown Airport and 20 minutes’ by car or 15-minutes via water taxi from Queenstown city centre. Guests can sample the best of New Zealand cuisine at the stylish Wakatipu Grill Restaurant. Cru Wine Bar & Lounge serves a wide range of local and international wines.


Rydges Lakeland Resort Queenstown

Offering stunning views over Lake Wakatipu and The Remarkables Mountains, Rydges Lakeland Resort features free WiFi, 2 on-site restaurants, an outdoor heated swimming pool and a sauna. Rydges Lakeland Resort Queenstown is situated on the shores of Lake Wakatipu, just 7 minutes’ walk from the heart of Queenstown. Queenstown Airport is a 15-minute drive away. The rooms all come with en suite bathrooms, a refrigerator and tea/coffee making facilities. Many rooms also come with a flat-screen TV and panoramic views of the lake. Rydges Lakeland Resort Queenstown has 165 guest rooms featuring 51 Superior Lake Facing rooms, 93 Deluxe Lake View Rooms and 21 One Bedroom Lake View Junior Suites.


Peppers Beacon

This property is 8 minutes walk from the beach. Peppers Beacon offers 5-star rooms and suites with views, on Lake Wakatipu in Queenstown. It is close to Skyline Gondola and Luge and just a 5-minute walk to the town centre. There is a selection of rooms and self-contained apartments available at Peppers Beacon, with many offering views of the lake and The Remarkables Mountain . Some accommodation includes a gas fireplace or a fully equipped kitchen. Guests can dine in the charming breakfast lounge or make use of the outdoor barbecue area. Peppers Beacon provides a sauna and hot tub for relaxation and a fitness room.


Scenic Suites Queenstown

This property is 4 minutes walk from the beach. Scenic Suites Queenstown is located in the resort of Queenstown. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from the centre, yet its quiet, hillside location offers great views over the lake and mountains. Free on-site parking is available to all guests. There is a restaurant at the property which is open for breakfast and dinner. There is also a lobby bar featuring a large open fireplace. Guests can choose between the spacious superior rooms or the large suites which offer a separate living room and kitchen facilities. All of the rooms are modern, neatly furnished and include en suite facilities. Guests enjoy complimentary WiFi
